Positano

Positano is a cliffside village on the Amalfi Coast, known for its pebble beachfront and steep, narrow streets lined with boutiques and cafes. The Church of Santa Maria Assunta features a majolica-tiled dome and a 13th-century Byzantine icon of the Virgin Mary. Positano is a popular holiday destination with a unique charm.
