Sorrento

Sorrento is a coastal town in southwestern Italy, facing the Bay of Naples on the Sorrentine Peninsula. It's known for sweeping water views and Piazza Tasso, a cafe-lined square. The historic town is filled with Renaissance palaces, a baroque cathedral, and the narrow alleys of the old town. Sorrento is also famous for its production of limoncello liqueur.
